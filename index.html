Restaraunt Bill Splitter

How to Use

Use by first entering the number of people in your party. When you have entered the number of people, press the "Enter" button and text fields will appear for you to enter in each person's name and the price of that person's meal. In addition, enter in the final price of the entire bill. Hit the "enter" button below and you will see how much each person's correct payment is after adjusting for tax and tip.
